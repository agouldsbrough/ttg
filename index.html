The Third Glass

A candlelit duel of hidden pours, poisoned cups, and one silent witness.

Current turn Player 1
Phase Place or drink
Round 1
Rule Summary

Goal

The Poisoner tries to kill a player with Poison. If the Silent Guest dies, roles are revealed and Final Toast begins.

Turns

Each player has one drawn card. Click a destination glass to place that card secretly, then draw a new card.

Glasses

There are three glasses: Player 1, the Silent Guest, and Player 2. Every card is 1 unit.

Drinking

When a glass reaches 5 units, it is drunk. Reveal 2 random cards from that glass.

Poison

If Poison is revealed, the drinker dies unless Antidote is also one of the two revealed cards. Player death ends the game.

Survival

If the drinker survives, empty that glass and shuffle every card in it back into the draw deck unseen. Play continues.

Hidden Info

You can see your own drawn card, but not the other player or computer's card.

Computer Mode

In Play computer mode, Player 2 acts automatically using a simple prototype strategy.
