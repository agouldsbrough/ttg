Rule Summary

Goal

The Poisoner is randomly assigned to Player 1, Player 2, or the Silent Guest. Guests win as a team if the Poisoner dies.

Turns

Each player has one drawn card. Click a destination glass to place that card secretly, then draw a new card.

Glasses

There are three glasses: Player 1, the Silent Guest, and Player 2. Any one of them may secretly be the Poisoner.

Drinking

When a glass reaches 5 units, it is drunk. Reveal 2 random cards from that glass.

Poison

If Poison is revealed, the drinker dies unless Antidote is also one of the two revealed cards. Player death ends the game.

Survival

If the drinker survives, empty that glass and discard every card in it unseen. Play continues.

Hidden Info

You can see your own drawn card, but not the other player or computer's card.

Computer Mode

In Play computer mode, Player 2 acts automatically using a simple prototype strategy.
